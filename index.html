The Escape

A Sanctuary in the Heart of Joypur

Welcome to Banashree Resort, a luxury nature retreat nestled in the dense greenery of Joypur Forest, West Bengal. Here, city families escape to breathe again, surrounded by birdsong mornings and bonfires under the stars.

Our resort is designed to blend seamlessly with nature, offering an eco-luxury experience without compromising on comfort. Guests come for the profound peace, and they stay for the feeling of being truly held by nature.
